4. **Embarrassment vetoes (all three, founder's words):**

     robot imagery, glassmorphism kitsch

   - "Boring corporate" — safe SaaS beige

## Synthesis: WARM MYSTIQUE

Dark and minimal, but the dark of a quiet room at night where you tell
someone your idea — not a cold void. Confidence through restraint.
Inviting through language, not decoration.

- **Canvas:** near-black with warmth (warm charcoal, never blue-cold)
- **Text:** warm cream/bone, generous line-height; one accent only —
  a candle-flame amber, used like the senate uses gold: sparingly
- **Type:** editorial serif for the voice (the interview is a conversation),
  restrained mono for the machinery underneath (the Roman steel showing
  through in details: article numbers, fixture IDs, costs)
- **Imagery:** none. No robots, no gradients, no illustrations. The words
  carry it. Negative space is the design.
- **Copy voice:** speaks TO the ideator, quiet and certain. Short sentences.
  The site should feel like the first line of the interview already began.

## Hard vetoes (Senate: reject on sight)

Purple/indigo gradients · neon glow effects · robot/brain imagery ·
glassmorphism cards · stock corporate anything · emoji as brand elements ·
exclamation-mark marketing copy
